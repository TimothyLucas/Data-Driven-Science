Just about everything about Apple's recent crop of 2016 and 2017 MacBook Pros is great. And the fact these models only come with USB-C ports is also a little divisive, but Apple isn't likely to add all the ports from older models back onto its upcoming laptops. The major thing that's getting a lot of negative attention is Apple's new "butterfly"- style keyboard on the newer 2016 and 2017 models.
