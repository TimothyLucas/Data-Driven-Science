A New York security guard was videotaped over the weekend brutalizing a would-be teenage thief who was accused of stealing a pair of Prada shoes. Wilson Acosta, who works at the 21 Century store in Lower Manhattan, and robbery suspect Victor Roberson were both arrested by the NYPD on Friday evening following a violent altercation. Filmed by a concerned bystander, the clip shows Roberson, 19, pinned down to the sidewalk by three private security employees of the store as the teenager struggles to scream 'I can't breath.'
