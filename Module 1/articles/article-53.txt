A white woman has been caught on tape calling the police on a black family as they set up for a barbecue at a park in Oakland, California, sparking outrage in the community. The unidentified woman in the viral video claims that the family were using a charcoal grill instead of a non-charcoal grill in one of Lake Merritt's designated grilling zones. A bystander named Michelle Snider, noticed the commotion and confronted the woman for 'harassing' the family, camera in hand, to record the 25-minute verbal altercation.
